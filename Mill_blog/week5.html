Gaining Velocity

Last watched a few videos about vector fields and how they affect different types of simulations. I was working 

Volume slice of the side view

Volume slice of the front view

To mimic the difference in pressure created by a fast moving object,  I created two velocity fields. I used shrinkwrap and remesh node to obtain lower-poly geometry for simulation purposes. I then used the peak node to displace the low-poly geometry away from the high-poly. I then used different noises and products to create a velocity field that pushes particles in toward the train. I later created another velocity field using then non-peaked points. To mix the values within both fields, I used multiple attribute paint and noises to create a normal vector for each point. I convert the normal attribute to velocity prior to rasterizing the volume.
