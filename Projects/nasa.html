NASA x SCADpro

I am very fortunate to be participate in this collaboration between NASA and SCAD via the SCADpro program. Over the course of two quarters (September 2022 - March 2023) I was able to work with Tom Neumann, Valerie Casasanto, Adriana Manrique, and JP Swinski to create various educational and data visualization projects related to ICESat-2 satellite.
